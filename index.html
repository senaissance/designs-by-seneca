The process

How it works

Here's what you can expect when you work with me.

Step 01

We talk

We start with a free conversation where I ask a few questions and we figure out if we're a good fit. You tell me about your business, what you need, and what's been holding you back.

01
Step 02

I design

Once we agree on a direction, I get to work. You'll see the design before anything is built, so you can give feedback and we can adjust so you get the results you want.

02
Step 03

We refine

This is where I hear your first impression and feedback to find out what's working and what isn't. We go through a round of revisions together, and I walk you through everything, welcoming your input.

03
Step 04

You launch

Your site goes live! I'll make sure you know how to use it and feel confident about it before I hand it over. I'll be there for the whole process until you feel comfortable with the end result.

I'm a designer and web builder based in California. I got into this work because I kept seeing people with really great ideas get stuck, and I have the technical skill and creativity to give life to their ideas.
